Solving the Rubik's Cube

All you need to finally solve the cube

Solving the Rubik’s cube is one of those things I had always thought would be a cool thing to do. Just not enough to actually do it.

If you’ve thought of solving the cube in the past but didn’t know where to start, this article will show you all you need to solve it.

In order to solve the cube I followed this video by WIRED. I recommend you use the video and this article together, refering to the more structured format of this article if ever you get lost or need some more details.

I tried to explain in this article all the elements I found confusing when first solving the cube. And seeing as I first solved it 2 weeks ago, your experience shouldn't differ too much.
